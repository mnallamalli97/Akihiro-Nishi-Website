Research
Nishi Lab at UCLA focuses on social networks, gamification, and human behavior. Although the home department of PI Nishi is Department of Epidemiology at Fielding School of Public Health, our research is not limited to public health, epidemiology or health behavior. The present keyword of the lab is visibility of socioeconomic status and that of human behavior. The present research projects include online social network experiments using breadboard (breadboard.yale.edu), promotion of motorcycle helmet use using social media in developing countries, and science of science.
