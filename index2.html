Opportunities

For UCLA Students:

Our lab accepts graduate and undergraduate students at paid positions (e.g. graduate student researcher) and unpaid positions (e.g. student volunteer). Our lab consists of four teams (administration, planning, analysis, and web development). Any skills related to these are a plus. If you are interested, please send your CV to the director of our lab (akihironishi@ucla.edu) and to the coordinator of our lab ( chrisagerman@gmail.com).




For Future Postdocs and Students:

Our lab accepts innovative and creative researchers at any stages (postdocs, graduate students, and undergraduates). Especially for the prospective postdocs and PhD students, please send your CV to the director of our lab (akihironishi@ucla.edu).
