Meet Our Team

Akihiro Nish


Principle Investigator


​Akihiro Nishi is the director of Nishi Lab at UCLA, and an Assistant Professor at UCLA Epidemiology. He is a physician and a social epidemiologist by training with expertise in network science and behavioral intervention. He holds a medical degree from University of Tokyo, a doctor of public health degree from Harvard School of Public Health. He was an awardee of JSPS Overseas Research Fellowship.
